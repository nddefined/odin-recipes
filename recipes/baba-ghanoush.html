Baba Ghanoush

Description

This is a recipe for baba ghanoush, a Levantine dip of cooked eggplant, olive oil, lemon juice, tahini, and various seasonings.

Baba ghanoush is eaten widely throughout the Levant. It is a nutritious dish, high in fiber and antioxidants, and contains heart-healthy fats and various vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1.5 tablespoons tahini sauce
  • 0.5 medium lemon, juiced
  • 0.5 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch dried parsley flakes
