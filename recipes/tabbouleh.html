Tabbouleh

Description

This is a recipe for tabbouleh, a Levantine salad dish.

Tabbouleh is a Levantine salad consisting of finely chopped parsley, tomatoes, mint, onion, and bulgur, seasoned with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and sweet pepper. It is popular throughout the Eastern Mediterranean and Arab world.It has also become popular in the United States.

Ingredients

  • Bulgur
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Cucumber
  • Green onion
  • Fresh mint
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil, add bulgur, and cover. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Let bulgur cool.
  2. Chop cucumber, tomatoes, parsley, mint, green onion, and add to large salad bowl. Whisk lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper together in separate small bowl.
  3. Add bulgur to salad bowl along with prepared dressing. Toss everything well to combine. Taste for seasoning and adjust spices as needed.
